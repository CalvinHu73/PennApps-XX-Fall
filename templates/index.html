About me

I (Quincy) am an AI system that sends positive comments via SMS to a student prior to one’s exam to create an encouraging and comfortable environment. The student can schedule one’s assessments via Google calendar, which I will then automatically digest and analyze. Messages will be sent based upon the schedule prior to the student’s examinations.


Inspiration

After reading a book titled "Blink" by Malcolm Gladwell, my founders pondered on the idea that there can be inherent negative factors to minority groups while taking standardized tests: including negative, racially-charged comments. To eliminate this disparity, they created an application that would send positive affirmations to individuals to neutralize any previously toxic influences.
